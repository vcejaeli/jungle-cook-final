Supreme Pizza

Description:

Make pizza night super duper out of this world with homemade pizza. This recipe is supreme with vegetables and two types of meat. Yum!

Total Time:

1h 24min

Servings:

4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 batch pizza dough
  • 2 tablespoons Last-Minute Pizza Sauce
  • 10 slices pepperoni
  • 1 cup cooked and crumbled Italian sausage
  • 2 large mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sliced black olives
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. 1. Preheat the oven to 475°. Spray pizza pan with nonstick cooking or line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. 2. Flatten dough into a thin round and place on the pizza pan.
  3. 3. Spread pizza sauce over the dough.
  4. 4. Layer the toppings over the dough in the order listed.
  5. 5. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes or until the crust is crisp and the cheese melted and lightly browned.
